/ About AGZUS

We are a team of
engineers, designers
and operators.

AGZUS Technology Solutions was founded on a simple thesis: the next decade of software will be won by the teams who can move from sketch to production without losing their soul. We help ambitious organisations do exactly that.

/ 01 — Principles

The principles that guide our craft.

/ Mission

Engineer the foundation of an intelligent enterprise.

We build the systems other companies depend on — data, identity, AI and the quiet infrastructure that makes the front-end possible.

/ Vision

A web that's open, fast, and resilient by default.

Products built on principles, not patches. Architectures that survive their second hire and their tenth customer.

/ Values

Taste, rigour and a bias for shipping.

We care about the details no one else will notice — because the compounding effect of small things is the whole game.

/ 02 — Journey

From two engineers to a global practice.

2019

Founded in Bengaluru.

Two founders, one rented desk, and a thesis that engineering services could be a craft.

2021

First enterprise contract.

Shipped a regulated payments platform. Hired the first 20. Learned what scale really costs.

2023

Opened Dubai & London.

Distributed engineering, follow-the-sun delivery and a real 24/7 on-call practice.

2026

Today — and the next decade.

220 engineers, 48 enterprise clients, and an AI practice that ships behind real products.
