/ 02 — FAQ

Common questions.

What's a typical engagement size? +

From 6-week sprints to multi-year platform partnerships. Most engagements involve an embedded pod of 3–8 people for 6+ months.

Do you do fixed-bid work? +

Rarely. We prefer time-and-materials with weekly demos and an opt-out at the end of every sprint.

Who owns the IP? +

You do. Always. We keep no rights to client work or derivatives.

Are you NDA-friendly? +

Yes. We'll sign yours, or send ours. Pre-call NDA is fine.

Where are your engineers based? +

Bengaluru is the largest hub. We staff across timezones to give every client four hours of meaningful overlap.

Compliance & security posture? +

SOC 2 Type II. ISO 27001. HIPAA-ready. Security review pack available under NDA.
